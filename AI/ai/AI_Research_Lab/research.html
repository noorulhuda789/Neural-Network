Our Projects

  • Laptop-Monitor Screen Classifier

    This project involves developing an image classifier from scratch in Python using only NumPy, to distinguish between laptop and monitor screens. We collect and preprocess a diverse dataset, implementing resizing and normalization with NumPy. A custom neural network, including convolution, pooling, and fully connected layers, is built and trained using gradient descent and backpropagation coded from scratch. After training and evaluating the model, the project concludes with a command-line interface for real-time image predictions, demonstrating the classifier's practical application.
